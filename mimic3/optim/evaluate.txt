Method: Scale learning of SGD by cosine-similarity, compare with and without learning rate decay.
Rationale: Smaller the vector similarity between batch and the mean, smaller then learning rate, thus the update.
----
checkmark is applied if with cosine scaling, either max f1 or mean f1 if is better in comparison.
no checkmark, when it is equal or worse.
